About Us

PharmaKraft Packaging Pvt Ltd is a leading provider of innovative packaging solutions for the pharmaceutical industry. We are dedicated to delivering high-quality products and exceptional customer service. Pharmakraft Packaging Private Limited, based in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, Maharashtra, was incorporated on November 18, 2020. The company specializes in the manufacture of paper and paper products. It operates under the corporate identification number (CIN) U21093PN2020PTC196091 and is classified as a private limited company limited by shares.

Pharmakraft Packaging provides a variety of packaging solutions, focusing on quality and innovation to meet the needs of its clients in various industries. The company is committed to sustainable practices and aims to deliver eco-friendly packaging solutions.
